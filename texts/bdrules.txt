---melee
When you **attack an enemy in melee**, roll+Strength. On a 10+, you deal your damage to the enemy and avoid being damaged. You may choose to deal +1d6 damage and expose yourself to the enemy attack. On a 7-9, you deal your damage to the enemy and the enemy makes an attack against you.
---ranged
When you take aim and **attack an enemy at range**, roll+Presence. On a 10+, your shot is clear, deal your damage. On a 7-9, choose one, and then deal your damage.
• You have to move to get the shot placing you in a danger of the GM’s choice.
• You have to take what you can get, roll damage twice and take the lower result.
• You have to take several shots, reducing your ammo by one.
---evade
When you **act quickly despite an immediate attack or danger**, roll+Sharp. On a 10+, you do it. On a 7-9, in the doing of it there will be a cost, complication, or choice introduced by the GM.
---endure
When you **endure a hardship that might kill a weaker soul**, roll+Resolve. On a 10+, you endure but choose three. On a 7-9, choose two.
• You take little or no harm.
• You don’t lose track of something important.
• You need little time to recover.
• Nothing feels your suffering and reacts to it.
---investigate
When you **closely study a situation or person**, roll+Sharp. On a 10+, ask the GM three questions from the list below. On a 7-9, ask only one. Take +1 forward when acting on the answers.
• What happened here recently?
• What is about to happen?
• What should I be on the lookout for?
• What here is useful or valuable to me?
• Who’s really in control here?
• What here is not what it appears to be?
---recollect
When you **recall past experiences about something**, roll+Resolve. On a 10+, the GM will tell you something relevant and useful about the subject at hand. On a 7-9, the GM will reveal an unwelcome truth about the subject at hand.
---words of power
When you **invoke the power of a scroll you have communed with**, roll+Presence. On a 10+, you successfully unleash the scrolls power without consequence. On a 7-9, this scrolls power is unleashed but choose one:
• You draw unwelcome attention or put yourself in a spot. The GM will tell you how.
• The scroll saps your energy. You take -1 ongoing to Presence until you have a few minutes to clear your head.
• After the scroll's power is unleashed, its power is forgotten, you cannot invoke its power until the next time you commune.
---humanity fractured
When you **inflict *humanity harm*** on someone, they face a hard truth about themselves or their beliefs. They either will not face it, or the act of them doing so has a harmful impact.
When you **take *humanity harm***, mark one humanity and choose one:
• You lose moments/minutes/hours as decided by the fiction.
• Must answer any question posed to you.
• Take disadvantage forward.
---defy death
When you’re dying and you catch a glimpse of what lies beyond this miserable life, mark one humanity and roll+Resolve. On a 10+, you’ve cheated death, you’re in a bad spot but you’re still alive. On a 7-9, a sacrifice is needed. Make a considerable enough sacrifice and stabilize or refuse and pass beyond into whatever fate awaits you. On a miss, your fate is sealed. You’re marked as Death’s own and you’ll cross the threshold soon. The GM will tell you when.
---evolve
When you have five humanity, you may subtract five at any time and come up with a move with your GM that you think would be appropriate for the strife your character has been through.
---rest
When you **settle in to rest and consume a ration**, as long as long as you get at least a few hours of uninterrupted sleep, heal damage equal to half your max HP.
---commune
When you spend uninterrupted time (an hour or so) in **quiet contemplation** with your scrolls, you are granted the ability to invoke the power of these scrolls.
---encumbrance
When you **make a move while carrying weight up to or equal to Load**, you’re fine. When you make a move while carrying weight equal to load+1 or load+2, you take -1. When you make a move while carrying weight greater than load+2, you have a choice: drop at least 1 weight and roll at -1, or automatically fail.
---unclean scrolls
1. **Missiles of Arcane Imperfection** - Projectiles of pure magic spring from your fingers. Deal 2d4 damage to one target.
2. **Begone Crude and Lesser Magics** - A spell or magic effect in your presence is torn apart. Lesser spells are ended, powerful magic is just reduced or dampened so long as you are nearby.
3. **You Are All Beneath Me Now** - You are capable of levitating and floating through the air at your own discretion. Keep in mind some actions may be more difficult while floating.
4. **Murmured Truths of the Universe** - You inflict humanity harm upon everyone around you.
5. **Cunning Tongue Of Mephistopheles** - Confuse and charm someone you touch until they take damage or you prove otherwise.
6. **Unknot the Sun In a Brilliant Blaze** - Unleash a ball of fire inflicting 2d6 damage on its target.
7. **Foul Magics Bring You Back From Beyond** - Invoke a hungry spirit to animate a dead body to fight for or protect you.
8. **Metzhuotl Blind Your Eye** - someone you touch becomes invisible for as long as you concentrate or they make an attack.
9. **Eyelid Blinds the Mind** - 1d4 creatures fall asleep for up to an hour.
10. **Cloud of Death’s Unholy Breath** - A cloud of fog drifts into this realm from beyond, filling the immediate area. Whenever a creature in the area takes damage it takes an additional, separate 1d6 damage which ignores armor.
---sacred scrolls
1. **Bestow the Prowess of Alexaniir Himself** - A combatant of your choice takes +1 ongoing so long as battle continues and they stand and fight.
2. **Grace of a Long Dead Angel** - At your touch, heal an ally you touch of 2d8 damage.
3. **Bless My Blade To Strike Good and True** - The weapon you hold while invoking this scroll does +1d4 damage until you dismiss it.
4. **Creatures Earth, Sky and Sea, Commune With Me** - You may speak with beasts.
5. **Bring the False Dawn / Ceaseless Night** - Summon blinding light or supernatural darkness.
6. **Clear Minded, Eyes Unblinded** - Your vision is opened to the true nature of everything you lay your eyes on. You pierce illusions and see things that have been hidden. The GM will describe areas before you ignoring any illusions and falsehoods, magical or otherwise.
7. **Consume the Life Which Should Be Not** - The mindless undead creature you touch is destroyed and you steal its death energy to heal yourself or the next ally you touch. The amount of damage healed is equal to the HP that the creature had remaining before you destroyed it.
8. **Whispers Pass the Grand Gates** - Name the spirit you wish to contact (or leave it to the GM). You pull that creature through the planes, just close enough to speak to you. It is bound to answer any one question you ask to the best of its ability.
9. **Unmet is that Fate** - One creature, dead for no more than a week, is awakened with terrible memories.
10. **The Divine Law Is In My Voice** - One creature obeys a single command.
---reliable
Once per battle you may reroll a single damage roll (yours or someone else’s).
